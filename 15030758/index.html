Contours and Elevation Model

The contours show the elevation of your area of interest and any variation therein. This can be used to determine what crops to plant and farm/construction practices to use.

Vegetation

Normalized Difference Vegetation Index

Our vegetation index is a simple and effective way to quantify healthy versus unhealthy or non-existing vegetation. It is a representation of how leaves reflect in satellite near-infrared and infrared waves. As a result, the maps show where vegetation is present and how healthy it is, what area is built up or exposed rock and what is water body.

Moisture

Normalized Difference Water Index

The moisture index is indicative of moisture in vegetation and exposed soil up to 5 cm deep, which shows how much water is available to (potential) vegetation. It also shows water bodies. The index is based on how water absorbs specific satellite waves.

Surface temperature

Ground surface temperature is a measure of how hot/cold the earth feels to the touch and is important in selecting what crop to plant and how severe a drought was/is.
